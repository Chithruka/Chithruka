Age Rating Guide

At Chithruka, we want to ensure you have the best viewing experience. Understanding age ratings helps you decide what is suitable for you and your family. Below is a comprehensive guide to the symbols you will see on our platform.

BBFC Ratings (UK Standard)

Universal (U)

Suitable for all. Ages 4+.

Parental Guidance (PG)

General viewing, but some scenes may be unsuitable for young children.

12A

Generally suitable for 12+. Under 12s must be accompanied by an adult.

12

Suitable for 12 years and over.

15

Suitable only for 15 years and over.

18

Suitable only for adults. 18+ only.

MPAA Ratings (US Standard)

General Audiences (G)

All ages admitted. Nothing that would offend parents.

Parental Guidance (PG)

Some material may not be suitable for children.

PG-13

Parents Strongly Cautioned. Some material inappropriate for children under 13.

Restricted (R)

Under 17 requires accompanying parent or adult guardian.

Adults Only (NC-17)

No one 17 and under admitted. Clearly adult.

Adults Only (X)

Historical rating replaced by NC-17.
